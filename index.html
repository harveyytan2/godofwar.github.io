INTRODUCTION

Starting with the release of God of War in 2005 on Playstation 2, Santa Monica Studio has created seven games accross multiple platforms up to the most recent release in 2018. Now comes the sequel to the critically acclaimed God of War (2018), God of War Ragnarok.
Join Kratos and Atreus on a mythic journey for answers before Ragnarök arrives. Together, father and son must put everything on the line as they journey to each of the Nine Realms.

All the while, Asgardian forces assemble…
